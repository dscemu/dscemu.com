Developer Student Clubs EMU is a prominent student developer community in North Cyprus. A Developer Students Club is a Google recognized student body at a college which directly reports to Google for their activities and is supported by the tech giant. A DSC conducts workshops, seminars and other fun activities which help the students connect with Google.

What we do?

Developer Student Clubs (DSC's) are university based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies.

Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcome. By joining a DSC, students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
